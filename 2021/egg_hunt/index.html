Egg hunt

Today I played egg hunt with my parents.

I played egg hunt at home this year because there is a virus and it is not safe outside. I played it with my mom and dad. One person hides the eggs and the other two try to find them. We take turns hiding the eggs. Sometimes me, mom or dad hides the eggs in funny places, like when I hid eggs inside a leg of a giraffe craft, when I hid an egg on top of a craft fish and when mom hid an egg in a fake flower in my dad’s bathroom.

I drew a card for my mom. First, it is an easter egg on the grass. Then, if you pull up the top the egg cracks and an easter bunny is in it! There are two flowers beside the egg. I also drew a card for my dad. It has the words “Happy Easter Day!” on it. The ‘D’ in Day was a carrot! on the ground there is the easter bunny with a basket of eggs. there is also some flowers an a tree on the ground. I’m proud of both of these cards.
