Going on a walk

Today I went on a walk with my mom. It was so fun!

Today my mom told me the weather good outside, so she asked me if I wanted to go outside. I said “Yes!!”. Then we dressed up and went outside. I saw white clouds in the blue sky. I walked with my mom and we walked to the metro station. We saw a lot of beautiful dandelions. They were big and yellow. I tried to count the petals, but there were to many.

We walked around the community. We saw a lot of flowers, like a big yellow one, a small purple one, a red tulip and cherry blossoms. I also saw medium sized white flowers, tiny golden flowers and pink blossoms.

My favorite flower was the small purple one because my favorite color is purple and because it looks like it is made of velvet.

When we were walking we didn’t see many people.
